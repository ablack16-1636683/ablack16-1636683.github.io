A website about my favorite place

The Getty Museum has spectacular views towards downtown, Santa Monica, and the beautiful Pacific Ocean, not to mention all the red brake lights on the 405 freeway. It has amazing gardens and art and the best part is the free admission!

Griffith Observatory is another great view spot but the exhibits here are more science related! The views here include the Hollywood sign and downtown, and at night have a look through the telescope at the starry sky

I threw this in because I am biased, but this hot dog stand is the best in LA, or anywhere! My great grandfather immigrated from Greece and started this stand which my grandfather then took over and all my aunts and uncles worked here growing up!
